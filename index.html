About Us

TechNova Solutions is a leading IT company founded in 2010, headquartered in Noida, Uttar Pradesh. We specialize in delivering cutting-edge software solutions to businesses across India and globally. Our team of 500+ engineers is committed to quality, innovation, and client satisfaction.

Our expertise covers web development, mobile applications, cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and enterprise software. We believe in building long-term relationships with our clients by providing reliable, secure, and scalable technology solutions.

Our mission is to empower businesses through innovative digital solutions while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and customer service.
